Scientific Strategy & Impact

I translate complex human behaviour into actionable organisational strategy. Specialising in Employee Engagement Analytics, I help companies move beyond "gut feel" to understand the rigorous "why" behind their people data.

  • Intervention & Programme Evaluation: Setting up rigorous evaluation studies to measure the real-world impact and ROI of cultural and behavioural interventions.
  • Research Scoping: Aligning business objectives with behavioural metrics to ensure you're measuring what matters.
  • Longitudinal Survey Set-up: Designing robust data collection frameworks to track cultural and engagement shifts over years, not just months.
  • Engagement Strategy: Interpreting complex data sets through a behavioural lens to identify high-impact "nudges" and cultural interventions.
